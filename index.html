Vikash Kumar Dubey

I'm Vikash — a Senior AI/ML Modeler Engineer at Sanofi in Hyderabad. Most of my day involves AI/ML modeling for clinical drug development — predicting how a disease might progress, figuring out which patients are the right fit for a clinical trial, and working with multimodal clinical data to support drug development decisions.

Before Sanofi, I spent four years at Cognizant working across healthcare and life-sciences projects — building data pipelines, implementing AI/ML models on clinical data, and putting together dashboards on AWS and Azure that clinical teams could actually use. Before that, I was doing medical AI research at the Institute of Liver & Biliary Sciences in Delhi, where I built AI/ML models for healthcare applications and developed deep learning models for medical imaging. That work got published, and it's what got me hooked on healthcare AI.

I mostly live in Python — PyTorch, XGBoost, Scikit-Learn, PySpark, SQL. I deal with clinical data standards (SDTM, ADaM) and the regulatory side (HIPAA, GDPR, 21 CFR Part 11) regularly. I've got 6 published papers across IEEE and APASL conferences, and between 2018 and 2020, I taught AI/ML and deep learning to about 1,000 students.

If you work in healthcare AI, clinical data science, or pharma AI/ML — or just curious about any of it — feel free to reach out.
